Biography

I joined Tencent in 2017 immediately upon graduation and currently work as a Researcher at the Robotics X Lab. My research focuses on Embodied AI and the multi-modal understanding of the dynamic (4D) physical world. My goal is to enable robots to perceive, reason, and interact seamlessly with complex environments and humans.

I obtained my Ph.D. from the Robotics Institute at HKUST in 2017, advised by Prof. Shaojie Shen. My doctoral research centered on robust state estimation, including visual odometry, 3D mapping, and SLAM. Prior to this, I honed my engineering skills at DJI, developing vision systems for UAVs, and conducted research on multimedia signal processing at MTrec under IEEE Fellow Prof. Oscar C. Au.

My technical roots lie in competitive programming (ACM-ICPC), which instilled in me a rigorous approach to problem-solving. Today, I combine that algorithmic discipline with deep learning to solve the next generation of challenges in robotic perception and interaction.
